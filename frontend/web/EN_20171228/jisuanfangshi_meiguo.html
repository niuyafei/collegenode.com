How we calculate the rankings

The ranking shows the high school's statistics for students to get into top universities. The top 5 universities are Stanford University, Harvard University, MIT, Princeton University and Yale University;

The top 10 universities include the top 5 adding Columbia University, University of Pennsylvania, Brown University, Darmouth College and Cornell University. The admission rate means the matriculation rate instead of the acceptance rate. One student can get accepted by multiple universities at the same time, but in the end can only matriculate in one school.
