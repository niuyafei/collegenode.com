1. Calculation Methods

The data is from the National Science Foundation, United States Department of Energy and Pomona College. The first data column shows the number of students holding an undergraduata degree from this institution, who received a Ph.D. degree during 2003-2012.

The second data column shows the total number of students that received a bachelor degree from the institution from 1998 to 2007.

The last data column shows the percentage of students who received a Ph.D. degree after graduating with a bachelor degree from this institution.

The rankings are given by different subjects. If you have a specific major in mind, you should check out which institutions are the best to help you get into a Ph.D. program in the future.
